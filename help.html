    Help - For Sellers

    Opening a store on GrubMarket is incredibly easy: it only takes a few minutes, and your sign-up process and listings are free!

    Here are 3 important things to do before opening your shop.

    1. Open a PayPal account. This is how you'll receive payments from your customers. Your payments will be deposited into your designated PayPal account. From your PayPal account, you can easily transfer money over to your bank account. Note that you do not have to pay to sign up for PayPal: simply choose the business plan that cost $0. Go here to sign-up: https://www.PayPal.com
    2. Take quality pictures of your food items for upload. Take at least a dozen photos and pick six for each item in your shop. You'll want to crop them into a square shape if possible. Here are some sites with expert tips on food photography: 1) http://content.photojojo.com/tips/food-photography-tips/ 2) https://www.udemy.com/blog/food-photography-tutorial/ 3) http://bakerbettie.com/easy-tips-for-improving-food-photography-for-beginners/
    3. Consider your delivery options. You have 5 options, and the options you select may depend on the type of food you are selling:
      1. Delivery by GrubMarket: Our GrubMarket team will pick up your items directly from your place, and then we will deliver it to the buyer on the same day. Prior to this delivery, our GrubMarket team will contact both you and the buyer to ensure both parties are available for the pick-up and receiving of the food. Our regular delivery range for Bay Area customers extends to six counties, including Santa Clara County, San Mateo County, Alameda County, San Francisco County, Contra Costa, and Marin County.
      2. Pick-Up at Your Store: Your buyers from GrubMarket will stop by your place of business to pick up the food. This is a very simple option and works great for buyers located near you.
      3. Pick-up at Agent: Find a local nearby establishment that will hold your food for a day or so for your buyer to pick it up. For example a convenience store or a farmers' market should work nicely.
      4. US Mail/UPS/FedEx: This is a great option for food that can be packaged for overnight delivery. UPS will even pick up your items at your place of business or home. (http://www.ups.com)
      5. Courier: This a great option if you want to hire a local courier, or you can simply deliver the items yourself to the buyers address.

    For shipping options, here are some sites that sell packaging food to avoid crushing/breaking or to preserve heat/cold: http://www.insulatedpackagingonline.com (thermal); http://www.plastifoam.com/index.php/catalog (protective); http://www.alibaba.com/showroom/fruit-boxes-for-shipping.html

    And now the easiest part: Opening your shop! Here are 3 quick tips to help make you successful:

    1. Select both PayPal and credit card for as your payment options. This gives the buyer more flexibility. Either way the payment goes directly to your PayPal account.
    2. Check your inbox and item reviews frequently, and respond promptly when necessary.
    3. Ship your orders promptly!

    Now click here to get started!

    Help - For Buyers

    How does GrubMarket work?

    1. Start by browsing our store. We have over 800 vendors all waiting to share their items with you. Simply add your desired items to your cart, and then proceed to checkout.
    2. Once your order is confirmed, our vendors will process your order and prepare your food.
    3. When your order is ready, our GrubMarket delivery team will contact you to arrange a convenient time for you to receive the delivery.
    4. Delivery day! On your delivery day, our team will deliver straight to your door. We will never deliver items picked up on a warehouse shelf, so that we can ensure that you always receive the freshest, high-quality food.

    How do I apply rebate codes?

    GrubMarket sometimes offers rebate codes which are post-payment rebates. Depending on your method of payment used to complete your order, our GrubMarket team will refund rebate offers through PayPal or by mailing a check within five business days.

    During checkout, you can enter the rebate code prior to submitting an order. After the payment is put through, you can view the amount of the rebate on the "Order Confirmation" web page, or "Order Confirmation" email.

    What if I want to add additional items to an existing order?

    You can choose to have as many items as you want delivered together as long as they are added before the cut off time for the delivery date of your outstanding order. Simply add items to your cart as you normally would and click checkout. Then you will be prompted to choose if you would like to have your items delivered together.

    The best part? There's no additional delivery fee for items delivered together!
