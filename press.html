    DELIVERY DETAILS
    The Farm Has Never Been Closer
    GrubMarket's mission is to conveniently make fresh and healthy food accessible to everyone. That's why you'll find prices up to 50% OFF traditional grocery stores - with delivery right to your door!
    Shop on GrubMarket Browse thousands of farm-fresh organic produce items and natural products. No subscriptions fees - just shop when you want.
    Order and Choose Delivery During checkout you'll select your preferred delivery day for your order.
    Receiving Your Order Sit back and relax - we'll send out our trusty drivers on your delivery day and your order will arrive right to your door!
    We make our regular deliveries 6 days a week, depending on your particular city. Since we work with amazing local farmers and artisan food markers, we may need a couple days to prepare your order. Below are the cutoff time for deliveries.
    Deliveries on Saturday, Monday or Tuesday
    Order by Wednesday at 10pm
    Deliveries on Wednesday, Thursday or Friday
    Order by Sunday at 10pm
    Delivery Time Window
    11am-8pm for most customers. Business addresses can select 9am-11am delivery.
    Delivery Cost
    Always FREE for orders over $39.99
    We also have an Express Market where you can order hundreds of items for "Next Day" delivery, 6 days a week. For most Bay Area cities you can place your order with Express Market items by 10pm and you'll receive it the following day.
